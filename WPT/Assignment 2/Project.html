Our Story

THE BULL THAT DEFIED THE WORLD

Ferruccio Lamborghini, a successful tractor manufacturer, founded Automobili Lamborghini in 1963 in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy. Driven by a personal dispute with Enzo Ferrari, he set out to build the perfect grand touring car.

What followed was more than a rivalry — it was the birth of a new automotive philosophy. Extreme design, raw power, and Italian craftsmanship fused into machines that defy convention and ignite passion in every soul that encounters them.

Today, Lamborghini is owned by the Volkswagen Group through Audi and stands as the pinnacle of super sports car engineering, entering a bold new era of electrification without sacrificing its soul.
